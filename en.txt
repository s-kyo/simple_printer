Talk about your neighbor
There is a kind women living next close to me, we have been neighbors for 10 years. 
When my family move to this street, she has already lived here for a long time. She used to be a teacher at a secondary school. She is 60 years old. 
Now She is living with her husband and 2 sons. She is short and thin. She has got a long black hair and brown eyes, she is warm and friendly, She likes cooking and watching TV. She always help me with my homework. We are really good neighbors.
